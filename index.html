About

Log 9 Materials Scientific Private Limited is a graphene research and development company. At Log 9 Materials we are determined to accelerate the commercialization of graphene nanotechnology. We have just developed the first commercial product on graphene- 'Smoke-safe'. This innovative graphene based cigarette filter reduces the risk of getting cancer by 90%. We offer a wide range of graphene (CVD graphene, rGO) and its derivatives. We bring to you graphene as researchers and not just manufactures, so we understand the triviality of the grade of our products.

Whys Choose Us

We love what we do, and we bring excellence and innovation to each product we work on. We are determined to provide our customers with the purest quality graphene and derivatives having the best chemical, optical, electrical and mechanical properties. We bring to you nano-materials as researchers and not just manufactures, so we understand the triviality of the grade of our products.

Foundation

The idea for the formation of Log 9 Materials Scientific Private Limited was conceived subsequent to the extensive research on graphene and its applications by the founders at Indian Institute of Technology, Roorkee. The development of new frontiers in graphene synthesis, reduction and functionalization with a focus on designing innovative applications paved the way for the foundation of this company.

Vision

To bridge the gap between graphene technology at labscale to amazing products with our focus on innovation and design. Log 9 Materials is not just a graphene manufacturer, but also a technology and design company with a focus on graphene based product innovation.
